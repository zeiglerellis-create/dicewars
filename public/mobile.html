On iPhone

  • Use Safari for the best full-screen experience.
  • Rotate to landscape if the board feels tight.
  • Share → Add to Home Screen for an app-like shortcut (optional).
  • Pinch zoom still works if you need it (up to 5×).

Controls

Setup: pick players, size, and options on the board, then Start. Play: tap hexes to place dice or attack. End turn sits on the board during battle.
